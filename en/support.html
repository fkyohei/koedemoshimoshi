Support (Hello! Pretend Phone)

"Hello! Pretend Phone" is an on-device pretend-phone app for children aged 0–3 and their families. Record your family's voices, and your little one gets a call from the people they love.

Contact

For questions, requests, or bug reports, please feel free to reach out:

Privacy

This app collects no personal information or usage data. Recordings never leave your device. See the Privacy Policy for details.
